Poison Ivy (Druid only)

Background

As someone, that is very keen with plants, you can distinguish good ones from poisonous ones.

You have the knowledge, to extract poisons from plants and coat your weapon with these.

Stats

  • Weapons: Slashing or piercing damage weapons that do not have the Gun-Property

  • Damage: +1D6 poison damage

Poison

You can hold up to 10 vials of poison and you carry the tools and plants, to refill the ones you have.

Every once in a while, you have to harvest fresh plants or visit a shop to restock your supplies. (When harvesting (takes 15 minutes), roll Nature Check: <11 -> 2 supplies, >=11 -> 5 supplies.)

While shortresting or just choosing to do so, you can refill 4+1D6 vials with posion. This process takes about 1 hour of your time.

Combat

To coat a weapon with poison in combat, you have to sacrifice 5 ft of movement speed on your current turn.

After coating your weapon with poison and not attacking for 1 minute, the poison will have dripped of the weapon.

If you attack unsuccessfully with a poison coated weapon, the poison will be swept away by the movement of the weapon.
