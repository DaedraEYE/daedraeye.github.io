Conjuring Wand (Clockwork Spider)

A little wand, around 30 cm long, that has a bronze brown color. Atop the wand are four dark green marble-like balls.

On the side of the wand is an engraving (Dwarven Language: "I require your help, little helper."). When said by the person wielding the wand, a blue sphere will appear on the ground in front of them. After about 4 seconds, the sphere will disappear and reveal a Clockwork Spider.

The spiders HP has to be rolled every time it is conjured. Upon conjuring the spider, the conjurer takes 10% (rounded up) of his max HP as damage.

When conjured in combat, the wand must be used as an action. The conjurer has his movement speed halfed (no movement that requires ability checks) and can't use his bonus action. The spider can't do anything in the round, it was conjured.

The spider is friendly towards its conjurer and will to what the conjurer says.

Clockwork Spider

PASSIVE:

Spider Climb: The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

ACTIONS:

Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 5 (1D8+1) piercing damage. If Bite hits, Stun is active.

While Stun is active, it is optional to use Bite again on the same target. Stun is deactive, while no target is bitten.

Stun: At the beginning of its round, the bitten target must take 3 (1D6) electic damage and make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or fall unconsious.

REACTIONS:

Grabbing: If someone or something wants to grab the spider, it can Bite (and instantly Stun) the one who tries to grab it. (Spider attacks with Disadvantage, if being grabbed from behind or above.)
